WELCOME TO KINGDOM
You are the Monarch. You will be presented with suggestions from various individuals. It is up to you to decide whether you say yes or no to their request. You must maintain the happiness of all animal subjects.

Click yes or no to accept or reject each suggestion. Every decision you make will raise or lower the human animal and non-human animal happiness levels at the top of the page. If one runs out, you lose. Refreshing will restart the game.
